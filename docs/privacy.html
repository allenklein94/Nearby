1. Who Can Use Nearby

Nearby is intended for adults only. You must be at least 18 years old to create an account. We verify your age at signup and do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will suspend the account and remove associated data.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: email address, display name, date of birth, and bio (optional).

Profile photo: the photo you upload is stored securely and automatically reviewed using an automated content moderation service before it becomes visible to other users. This review checks for prohibited content and looks for signals suggesting the photo may show someone under 18; if such a signal is found, your profile is kept hidden pending manual review by our team.

Location information: Nearby uses your device's location to determine whether you have recently been in close physical proximity to another user of the App. We only ever store a coarse, rounded approximation of your location (accurate to roughly 100 meters) — never your exact coordinates, and never a history or trail of your movements. This coarse location data is automatically deleted after one hour. Records of a "crossed paths" event between two users are automatically deleted after 48 hours.

Usage information: we keep records of notices sent, matches made, and messages exchanged, for as long as your account is active, so the App can function.

Push notification token: if you enable notifications, we store a device token used solely to deliver notifications about new matches and messages to you.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the core features of the App: showing you people you've crossed paths with, enabling mutual "Notices," creating matches, and enabling chat between matched users.
  • To moderate photo content and protect the safety of our community.
  • To send you push notifications about matches and messages, if enabled.
  • To respond to reports of abuse or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data to serve third-party advertising.

4. How We Share Information

With other users: your display name, bio, and profile photo are visible to other users you've crossed paths with, once your photo has passed moderation. Your exact location is never shown to any other user.

With service providers: we use third-party services to operate the App, including a database and backend hosting provider (Supabase), an automated content moderation and image analysis provider (Amazon Web Services), an email delivery provider (Brevo), and a payment processing provider (Apple, via in-app purchase, and RevenueCat for subscription management). These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

For legal reasons: we may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

5. Data Retention

  • Coarse location data used for matching is deleted automatically after 1 hour.
  • Records of a crossed-paths event are deleted automatically after 48 hours if no interaction occurs.
  • Messages and match history are retained while your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, we delete your profile information, photo, and associated data, except where retention is required for legal or safety reasons.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • Blocking: you may block another user at any time. Blocking is immediate and one-directional; the blocked user is not notified.
  • Reporting: you may report another user's profile or messages for review.
  • Notifications: you may disable push notifications in your device settings at any time.
  • Account deletion: you can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab within the App.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable law (such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data, or object to certain processing). Contact us using the information below to exercise these rights.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage, restricted database access controls, and automated content moderation. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
